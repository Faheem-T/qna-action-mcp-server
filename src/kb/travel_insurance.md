# Travel Insurance - GlobeTrotter
## Trip Cancellation
Reimburses up to 100% of your non-refundable trip costs if you must cancel for a covered reason (illness, injury, death in family, jury duty).

## Trip Interruption
Reimburses up to 150% of your unused trip costs if your trip is cut short due to a covered reason.

## Emergency Medical Expense
Covers up to $50,000 for medical treatment if you get sick or injured while traveling.

## Emergency Medical Evacuation
Covers up to $500,000 for transportation to the nearest adequate medical facility.

## Baggage Loss/Delay
- **Baggage Loss**: Up to $1,000 for lost, stolen, or damaged luggage.
- **Baggage Delay**: Up to $200 for essential items if bags are delayed more than 12 hours.

## 24/7 Assistance Services
- Medical referrals
- Legal assistance
- Lost passport help
- Translation services
