Underwriting Guidelines - Confidential

Risk Assessment Criteria:

1. Health History
   - Hypertension: Acceptable if controlled (<140/90). Refer to Medical Director if uncontrolled.
   - Diabetes: Type 1 requires A1C < 7.5 for Standard rates. Type 2 allows Preferred if A1C < 7.0.
   - BMI: Standard rates up to BMI 35. Over 35 requires table rating.

2. Lifestyle
   - Smoking: Must be nicotine-free for 12 months for Non-Smoker rates.
   - Aviation: Private pilots require Aviation Questionnaire. No coverage for stunt flying.

3. Financial
   - Income replacement ratio: Max 20x annual income for ages 20-40. 15x for 41-50.
   - Bankruptcy: Must be discharged for >2 years for Preferred rates.

Auto Underwriting Rules:
- Drivers under 25: Surcharge applies.
- DUIs: Decline if within last 3 years. Refer to non-standard pool if 3-5 years.
- Vehicle Type: High-performance vehicles require senior underwriter approval.
