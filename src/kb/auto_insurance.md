# Auto Insurance Policy - DriveSafe
## Coverage Types

### Liability Coverage
Pays for bodily injury and property damage that you cause to others.
- **Bodily Injury**: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
- **Property Damage**: $50,000 per accident

### Collision Coverage
Pays for damage to your car resulting from a collision with another car or object.
- **Deductible**: $500

### Comprehensive Coverage
Pays for damage to your car caused by something other than a collision (theft, fire, vandalism, hail).
- **Deductible**: $250

### Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist
Protect yourself if you're hit by a driver who doesn't have insurance or enough insurance.

### Medical Payments (MedPay)
Pays for medical expenses for you and your passengers, regardless of fault.

## Exclusions
- Use of vehicle for ride-sharing (unless endorsement added).
- Intentional acts.
- Racing or speed contests.
