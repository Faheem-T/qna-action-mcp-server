# Available Discounts

## Auto Insurance Discounts
- **Safe Driver**: Up to 30% off for drivers with no accidents or violations in the last 3 years.
- **Multi-Car**: 15% off when you insure two or more vehicles.
- **Good Student**: 10% off for students with a "B" average or better.
- **Defensive Driving Course**: 5% off for completing an approved course.

## Home Insurance Discounts
- **Bundling**: Save up to 20% when you bundle home and auto insurance.
- **Security System**: 5% off for homes with a monitored burglar alarm.
- **Smoke Detectors**: 2% off for homes with smoke detectors.
- **New Value**: Up to 10% off for new homes.

## Life Insurance Discounts
- **Preferred Health**: Lower rates for individuals in excellent health.
- **Non-Smoker**: Significant savings for non-smokers.
