# Homeowners Insurance - SafeHaven
## Dwelling Coverage
Covers the structure of your home (walls, roof, built-in appliances).
- **Limit**: $500,000

## Other Structures
Covers detached structures like garages, fences, and sheds.
- **Limit**: $50,000 (10% of Dwelling)

## Personal Property
Covers your belongings (furniture, clothes, electronics) anywhere in the world.
- **Limit**: $250,000 (50% of Dwelling)
- **Special Limits**: Jewelry ($1,500), Cash ($200).

## Loss of Use
Pays for additional living expenses if your home is uninhabitable due to a covered loss.

## Liability Protection
Protects you against lawsuits for bodily injury or property damage that you or your family members cause to other people.

## Perils Covered
- Fire and Smoke
- Lightning
- Windstorm and Hail
- Theft
- Vandalism
- Water Damage (from bursting pipes)

## Perils Not Covered
- Flood (requires separate policy)
- Earthquake (requires separate policy)
- Wear and Tear
- Pest Infestation
