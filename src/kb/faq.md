# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General
**Q: How do I access my policy online?**
A: Go to our website and click "Login". You can register using your policy number and date of birth.

**Q: Can I change my payment due date?**
A: Yes, you can change your payment due date by contacting customer service.

## billing
**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and electronic checks (EFT).

**Q: Is there a fee for paying by credit card?**
A: No, we do not charge a fee for credit card payments.

## Claims
**Q: How long does it take to process a claim?**
A: Simple claims are often processed within 48 hours. More complex claims may take several weeks.

**Q: Will filing a claim increase my premium?**
A: It depends on the type of claim and your policy history. Not all claims result in a premium increase.
