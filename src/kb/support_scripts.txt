Customer Support Scenarios and Scripts

Scenario: Customer wants to cancel policy.
Script: "I'm sorry to hear you're thinking of leaving us, [Name]. may I ask what has prompted this decision? Is there anything we could do to resolve the issue? I'd be happy to review your policy to see if we can find any discounts you might be missing."

Scenario: Customer is upset about a claim denial.
Script: "I understand this is frustrating news, [Name]. The decision was based on the exclusion for [Exclusion] found on page [X] of your policy. However, you do have the right to appeal this decision. Would you like me to explain the appeal process?"

Scenario: Customer asks about rate increase.
Script: "I see that your rate has changed. This adjustment is due to [Reason: general rate increase required by state / change in vehicle / ticket]. Let's look at your coverage options to see if we can adjust the plan to better fit your budget."
