# Claims Process
## How to File a Claim

### Step 1: Report the Loss
Contact us as soon as possible after the incident.
- **Online**: Log in to your account at www.securetomorrow.com/claims
- **Phone**: Call 1-800-555-0198
- **Mobile App**: Use the "File a Claim" feature.

### Step 2: Provide Documentation
You may be asked to provide:
- Police report (for theft or accidents)
- Photos of damage
- Receipts for damaged items
- Medical records (for injury claims)

### Step 3: Investigation
A claims adjuster will be assigned to your case. They will:
- Review your policy coverage.
- Assess the damage.
- Determine the amount payable.

### Step 4: Resolution
- **Approval**: If your claim is approved, payment will be issued via check or direct deposit.
- **Denial**: If your claim is denied, you will receive a letter explaining the reason.

## Tips for a Smooth Claim
- Take photos immediately after an incident.
- Keep an inventory of your personal property.
- Do not discard damaged items until the adjuster has seen them.
- Be honest and cooperative with the adjuster.
