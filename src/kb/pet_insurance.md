# Pet Insurance - FurryFriends
## Plan Overview
- **Reimbursement**: 80%
- **Annual Deductible**: $250
- **Annual Limit**: $10,000

## What is Covered
- Accidents and Injuries
- Illnesses (Cancer, Diabetes, Allergies)
- Hereditary and Congenital Conditions
- Diagnostic Tests (X-rays, Blood Tests)
- Surgeries and Hospitalization
- Prescription Medications

## What is Not Covered
- Pre-existing Conditions
- Cosmetic Procedures (Tail Docking, Ear Cropping)
- Breeding Costs
- Routine Wellness Care (unless Wellness Rider added)

## Waiting Periods
- **Accidents**: 3 days
- **Illnesses**: 14 days
- **Cruciate Ligament Issues**: 6 months
